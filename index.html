How to Play
  • Every 5 seconds, you can change the direction using the arrow keys. Or by swiping on mobile.
  • Bounce off the arena edge to create "ties".
  • Cut the enemy's ties by hitting them.
  • Protect your own ties from being cut.
  • If you have ties and the enemy has none, you win.
  • If the enemy has ties and you have none, you lose.
Good luck!
